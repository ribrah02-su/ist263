What is a Trinket?

A trinket is typically a small, decorative item collected for fun rather than for practical use. Some collectibles can also be considered trinkets, especially when they are small in size. Since most of the items I collect are tiny, I personally think of them as trinkets.

Examples of Trinkets and Collectibes

  • Coins
  • Currency notes
  • Antique and Vintage Toys
  • Miniature or novelty Toys
  • Shiny rocks and crystals

Why I began Collecting

I first got into collecting because I discovered Sonny Angels and thought they were adorable. I also loved the concept of blind boxes—the idea that you don’t know which figure you’ll get until you open it. There’s a fun element of surprise and chance: you might pull the exact one you were hoping for, or discover a new favorite. For me, the excitement of unboxing combined with the creativity and detail that goes into designing these little figures makes collecting really enjoyable.
