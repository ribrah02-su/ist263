Beginners Guide to Trinket Collection

Starting a trinket collection can be fun and rewarding, but there are a few things to consider before you begin:

1. Choose what you want to collect
Decide whether you want to focus on one type of collectible (for example, Sonny Angels) or branch out into different trinkets like Calico Critters, Sanrio items, or vintage toys.
2. Set a budget
Collecting can get expensive, so think about how much you’re comfortable spending. Your budget will help guide which types of trinkets make sense for you.
3. Decide how to handle duplicates
Many collectibles come in blind boxes, so you may get duplicates or items you don’t really want. Some collectors enjoy keeping them, while others prefer selling or trading them to refine their collection.
